Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to What's The Move ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using What's The Move, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account and use our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional), and age range
  • Event Data: Events you save, star, or interact with
  • Calendar Preferences: Your calendar sync settings and selected calendar
  • Notification Preferences: Your choices for event reminders and alerts
  • Device Information: Device token for push notifications, timezone
  • Analytics Data: Usage patterns and app interactions (only if you opt-in)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Send you notifications about events you've saved
  • Sync events with your device calendar (when enabled)
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant events
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app (only with your consent)
  • Communicate important updates about our services
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase, a Google Cloud service, to securely store and process your data. Your information is protected using industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Regular security assessments

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Notification Settings: Customize or disable push notifications at any time
  • Analytics: Opt-in or opt-out of analytics collection in app settings
  • Calendar Sync: Enable or disable calendar integration
  • Account Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data Access: Request a copy of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud messaging
  • Apple Calendar: Event synchronization (when enabled by you)
  • Google Calendar: Event synchronization (when enabled by you)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

What's The Move is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
